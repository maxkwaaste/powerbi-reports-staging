6.0 Frequently Asked Questions
Is the January 2026 decline seasonal or structural?

January typically dips after Q4 project close-outs, but this January (€771K) is 18.2% below January 2025 (€942K). That gap indicates some project revenue from 2024 was not replaced by new contracts in 2025. Look at whether new client acquisition in H2 2025 compensated for project completions.

Which clients need immediate attention?

Client D dropped 45.4% in one month. Client A, your largest account (€2.3M lifetime), fell 19.9%. Client H and Client I both dropped over 23%. These four accounts should have check-in calls scheduled this week.

Why did May 2025 have such a low margin despite high revenue?

May 2025 had €789K in cost against €1.08M revenue, producing a 27.0% margin. This was the lowest margin in 18 months. High cost months typically indicate large project deliveries, subcontractor billing, or overtime. Check the PSA time entries for that month to identify the cost driver.
